Beans

Description

Delicious beans cooked just using water and salt.

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Sort the beans very carefully. They usually contain little rocks that can hurt our theeth.
  2. Put enough boiling water in a cooking pot to completely cover your beans and mantain it in high heat.
  3. Leave them coocking for about three hours mixing and adding watter periodically
  4. Add the salt when the beans feel soft and the water had turned brown-ish.
  5. The beans are ready!
